Welcome to InteractiCA!

A series of interactive lessons in cellular automata.

What are Cellular Automata?

A cellular automaton is a collection of cells which have state and change over time. These changes are driven by the state of themselves and the cells around them.

Why should I learn about Cellular Automata?

The construct of cellular automata have been found in applications of many areas, from physics to biology to the behavior of avalanches and traffic flow. They can also be great way to get hands-on experience in some fundamental concepts of programming and Computer Science. If any of this sounds interesting, or if you just want to learn how the cool patterns you see above are created, Cellular Automata are a great thing to learn.
